三浦 建太郎

Kentaro Miura, a prolific Japanese manga artist, left an indelible mark on the world of storytelling through his acclaimed dark fantasy series, Berserk. Born on July 11, 1966, in Chiba, Japan, Miura's artistic prowess and storytelling abilities have inspired and captivated readers around the globe.

運命

  • 1966 – Born in Chiba, Chiba Prefecture, Japan
  • 1976 – Creates first manga, Miuranger, at the age of 10
  • 1985 – Enrolls in the art college of Nihon University
  • 1985 – Wins 34th Newcomer Manga Award
  • 1989 – Begins serialization of Berserk, at the age of 23
  • 2021 – Passes away on May 6, 2021, at the age of 54

遺産

Kentaro Miura's legacy extends far beyond his passing. Berserk, with over 60 million copies in circulation, stands as one of the best-selling manga series of all time. Miura's influence can be seen in various mediums, from games to anime, and his unique artistic style continues to inspire artists and creators worldwide.

夢

“Dreams. Each man longs to pursue his dream. Each man is tortured by this dream, but the dream gives meaning to his life. Even if the dream ruins his life, man cannot allow himself to leave it behind. In this world, is man ever able to possess anything more solid, than a dream?”

― Kentaro Miura, Berserk
